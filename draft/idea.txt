custom playlist
===========================================================
_id: id of the playlist
songs: [
    collection of songs (gets default songs schema)
]
totalSongs: no of songs 
artist: artistname | others
album: albumname | others
movie: [movienames| others]
language: language | mix | others
genre: Rock | Folk | Melody | Jazz | instrumental | pop | others
tags: [related tags for search purpose]
isActive: true | false
createdBy: _id of created person
createdAt: date of creation
updatedAt: date of updation
